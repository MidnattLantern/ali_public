About The Good Samaritan (TGS)

The Good Samaritan is a passion project by Alma and Emma. We collaborated on our graduation project: Baseball Animation , and decided to continue making projects together after graduation.

Emma Fyrmark went on to study screenwriting and is the writer and director of The Good Samaritan.

I am the illustrator and co-designer of each character, as well as the web developer for the webcomic.

The Good Samaritan is a slice-of-life teenage drama. We follow Sam as she meets new people and discovers more about herself.

The webcomic is still in the making. We invite you to follow along and track the progress!
